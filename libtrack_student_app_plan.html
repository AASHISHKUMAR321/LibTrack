# LibTrack Student App - Design Plan ## 1. Student Login - **Purpose:** Secure access for students. - **Fields:** Student ID or Registered Phone Number. - **CTA:** "Sign In". ## 2. Student Dashboard - **Purpose:** Core hub for students. - **Top:** Greeting and current status (e.g., "Inside Library" or "Checked Out"). - **Membership Card:** Quick view of active plan and days remaining. - **Quick Action:** Prominent "Scan QR to Check In/Out" button. - **Recent Notifications:** Preview of the last 2-3 alerts. ## 3. QR Attendance Scanner - **Purpose:** Functional screen for checking in/out. - **UI:** Active camera viewfinder with a scanning frame. - **Feedback:** Clear "Success" overlay after a valid scan. ## 4. Notifications Center - **Purpose:** List of all library updates. - **Content:** Membership expiry warnings, library holiday announcements, and check-in/out confirmations. ## 5. Student Profile & History - **Purpose:** Personal record keeping. - **Content:** User details, membership stats, and a full log of past attendance.
